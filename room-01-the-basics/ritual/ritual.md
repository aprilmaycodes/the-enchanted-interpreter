# The Claiming Ceremony

*The sigils on the walls of the first room glow faintly, stirring with recognition. The registry is filled. The oath is known. All that remains is to speak your claim aloud.*

*You stand at the center of the room. The air is still. The cottage is waiting.*

*It is time.*

---

## Objectives
Write a program that conducts the claiming ceremony. Your program should:

1. Ask for your name
2. Ask which tenet speaks to you most, of:
    - "I will speak clearly."
    - "I will know the nature of things."
    - "I will adapt and endure."
3. Output the full claiming ceremony document

## Example Output.
```
What is your name: April
The tenets are:

    - "I will speak clearly."
    - "I will know the nature of things."
    - "I will adapt and endure."

Which path do you choose?: I will know the nature of things

THE CLAIMING CEREMONY
=====================

Let it be known that April stands before the wards
of Willowmere and makes the following declaration:

	"I will speak clearly."
	"I will know the nature of things."
	"I will adapt and endure."

I, April, have chosen my path:
"I will know the nature of things"

By the old magic, it is done.
Welcome home, Keeper.
```
