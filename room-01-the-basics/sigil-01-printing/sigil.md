# Sigil 1: The Keeper's Promise

*The first room of the cellar is dusty and dim, but you're drawn to the sigils on the wall. As you move closer, letters begin to surface, as though the sigil is trying to communicate.*

*It's an inscription. An oath. And it seems to be waiting for you to speak it.*

---

## Objective
Decipher the sigil by printing the following exactly as shown - formatting and all.

```
THE KEEPER'S PROMISE
====================

The keeper of the wards must swear to abide by the laws of the old magic:

    "I will speak clearly."
    "I will know the nature of things."
    "I will adapt and endure."

Speak truly, and the old magic will hear you.
```
---
## Tips
- You can use multiple print statements, or just one - your call.
- You'll need escape sequences to get the formatting right. The indented lines use a tab, not spaces
