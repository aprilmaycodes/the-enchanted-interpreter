# The Letter

The sigil flickers.

It begins slowly, like a pulse, and then quickens as blue light brightens until the whole room is illuminated. You step back and take in what you couldn't see before. The room is larger than you'd thought. There are shelves here too, lined with jars and more books written in the same strange language as before.

On the floor, beneath the newly illuminated sigil, is an envelope with your name on it in your grandma's flowing handwriting. Your hands are steadier than you'd expect as you open it, and become less steady the more you read.

In the letter your grandma details that she was a witch. Her job was to maintain the wards of Willowmere that keep the land abundant and darker forces at bay. She used something called Pythonic magic - the strange language you saw in the journals. Now she is gone, and the responsibility falls to you. Learn the language and restore the sigils to maintain the wards. *You are the new keeper.*

You sit with the letter for a while, taking it all in. "Everything you need is in the cottage" she'd said. You stand, determined to master the magic language and do her proud.

Taking a deep breath, you make your way to the second sigil.
