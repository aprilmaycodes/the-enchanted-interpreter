# Sigil 2: The Keeper's Registry

*The second sigil is different from the first. Where it was faded, this one is simply blank — smooth stone where there should be markings. A small plaque beside it reads:*

*"The ward cannot know its keeper until its keeper is known."*

*Beside the blank sigil is an empty ledger, open to the first page. It is waiting to be filled in.*

---

## Objective
Write a program that fills in the keeper's registry by collecting information and displaying it in a formatted entry.

Your program should:
1. Ask for your name
2. Ask which tenet speaks to you most, of:
    - "I will speak clearly."
    - "I will know the nature of things."
    - "I will adapt and endure."
3. Output a formatted registry entry using the information collected.

## Expected Output.
```
What is your name: April
Welcome, April. The tenets are:

    - "I will speak clearly."
    - "I will know the nature of things."
    - "I will adapt and endure."

Which tenet speaks to you?: I will adapt and endure

KEEPER'S REGISTRY
=================

Name: April
Chosen Tenet: "I will adapt and endure"

This record has been added to the cottage archives.
The secong sigil stirs.
```
---

## Tips

- You'll need variables to store the keeper's responses
- Use an f-string to include the collected information in your output.
